Maps

Erangel

A classic 8x8 km map filled with forests, military bases, cities, and open fields. Popular drop spots include Pochinki, School, and Sosnovka Military Base.

Livik

A compact 2x2 km map designed for quick 10-minute matches. Combines snow, grass, and rocky terrain. Great for fast-paced gameplay

Sanhok

A 4x4 km jungle-themed map with thick trees, rivers, and temples. Expect close-range combat and surprise ambushes in the wild terrain.

Miramar

A desert-themed 8x8 km map focused on long-range combat and open land. Snipers dominate here. Key locations: Hacienda, Pecado, and Los Leones.

Vikendi

Vikendi is a snowy 6x6 map known for stealthy gameplay, mountains, and frozen lakes. Good mix of close and mid-range combat.
